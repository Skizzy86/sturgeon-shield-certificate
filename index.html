Coverage — What Is Protected

This Certificate constitutes a dealer-backed service warranty issued directly by Sturgeon Chrysler Dodge Jeep Ram ("the Dealer"). It is not a contract of insurance. Coverage is provided by way of minor repair or re-application of the protection products described herein, in respect of reasonable and expected wear and tear arising from normal vehicle use. Sections below apply only to the products included in the purchased package or add-on services.

Section A — Interior Protection (Rip · Tear · Burn) — Tier 3 / Total Control

Covers reasonable and expected wear-and-tear damage to treated leather, synthetic leather, cloth, and vinyl upholstery surfaces. Included in Tier 3 (Total Control) only.

  • Accidental rips, tears, cuts, or burns up to 5 cm (2 inches) in any direction that penetrate the surface layer of original factory-installed or factory-supplied upholstery.
  • Applies to front and rear seating surfaces, centre console lid, door panel fabric/leather inserts, and headrests where the Leather Interior Shield was applied.
  • Qualified damage will be repaired or, if the coating has failed to protect as warranted, the affected surface will be re-treated at no charge.
  • Coverage Term: 5 years from delivery date.
  • Maximum of three (3) service claims per 12-month period.

Section B — Exterior Paint Protection (Fading & UV Degradation) — All Tiers

Covers visible paint fading and UV-related colour degradation on treated exterior panels. Applicable to all Sturgeon Shield tiers.

  • Visible fading, chalking, or measurable loss of colour depth on factory-painted exterior body panels attributable to normal UV radiation and atmospheric exposure.
  • If the applied graphene or ceramic coating is found to have failed to provide its warranted UV-barrier and hydrophobic protection under normal use, the Dealer will re-apply the coating to affected panels at no charge.
  • Tier 1 (Essentials): 5+ years — Graphene Paint Armour coating.
  • Tier 2 (Essentials+): Permanent — Ceramic PRO 10H Shield provides long-term paint defect and UV coverage.
  • Tier 3 (Total Control): 10 years for new vehicles / 5 years for pre-owned — inclusive Paint Defect Coverage.
  • Applies to accessible, factory-painted vertical and horizontal sheet metal body panels, glass, and treated trim.
  • Does not replace or supplement the OEM factory paint warranty provided by Stellantis Canada.
  • Maximum of two (2) service claims per 12-month period.

Section C — Rust & Corrosion Protection

Covers surface rust and corrosion prevention on treated panels. Applicable to Tier 3 (Total Control) and/or when rust protection add-ons are purchased.

  • Electronic Rust Inhibitor (Tier 3): The OBD-II plug-in module generates a repetitive pulsing surface current, which reduces metal oxidation across the vehicle's sheet metal panels 24/7.
  • Rust Inhibitor Spray: Solvent-based amber product injected into inner cavities, door panels, and crevices. Bonds to metal, will not drip, displaces existing moisture. No drilling required. Guarantees against rust perforation occurring from the inside out.

  • If the undercoating is found to have failed to adhere or provide protection under normal use within the coverage term, the Dealer will reapply the affected section at no charge.

Exclusions — What Is Not Covered

This Certificate does not cover damage, loss, or failure arising from or related to any of the following:

  • Damage from collision, impact, vandalism, theft, fire, flood, hail, or any other external or fortuitous peril.
  • Use of non-approved cleaning products, abrasive polishes, industrial solvents, or improper washing or detailing techniques.
  • Rust perforation (rust-through, creating a hole in sheet metal) — covered separately under the Stellantis Canada Anti-Corrosion Perforation Limited Warranty.

Additional Exclusions

  • Coverage sections not included in the purchased package tier or as a confirmed add-on service on this Certificate.

How To Make A Claim

  • Contact Sturgeon Chrysler Dodge Jeep Ram Service Department at sturgeondodge.ca or by calling the dealership to schedule a claim inspection.
  • Present this Certificate along with a valid government-issued photo ID and proof of vehicle ownership.
  • Describe the damage or coating failure. The service advisor will assess whether it falls within the coverage criteria for the purchased tier and add-ons.
  • If the claim is approved, the Dealer will perform the repair or re-application at no charge, subject to the applicable claim limits.
  • Unauthorized repairs or re-applications performed by third parties will not be reimbursed and may void remaining coverage.
